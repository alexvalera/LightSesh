    LightSesh is a study framework designed to help students budget their time and focus on their tasks one at a time.



    LightSesh uses a 30 minute countdown timer that is divided into three sections in order to boost productivity:

    Study. Review. Break.

    You can add what you need to work on to the "Missions" panel. Also, if needed, you can take notes in the "Notes" panel.
